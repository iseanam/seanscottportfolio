About

Officially personal.

I’m interested in how digital systems influence daily life, behaviour, culture, and connection. My studies at Maastricht University let me explore technology not only as a tool, but as something deeply social.

Outside university, I enjoy movement and focus: tennis, badminton, rock climbing, and bouldering. I also hold a drone license, which reflects my interest in precision, perspective, and exploring the world from new angles.

I prefer a balanced lifestyle. I don’t smoke or drink, and I take my health seriously. For me, fun is about being fully there: movie nights, good conversations, shared plans, and meaningful time with Carla.

I’m drawn to clean design, thoughtful experiences, and building a life that feels both grounded and creative.
